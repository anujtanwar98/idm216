User

Chad Bolton, D1 athlete, age: 20

Scenario

His teammates are very excited about getting food at Tyson's Bees. He is a regular of the food truck. He is hesitant to go during lunch time because he knows how busy it is. Then his friends tell him that he can order the food online and it will be ready when he arrives at the food truck.

Goal

He wants to make sure that he can meet his dietary needs within a small time frame, between practice and class.
